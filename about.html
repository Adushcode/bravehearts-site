About Brave Hearts

Compassionate cancer support founded by medical professionals

Our Story

Brave Hearts Against Cancer was founded in 2023 by Ronald Aduda, a registered clinical officer with a passion for oncology breakthroughs. Despite only practicing for 2 years as a general practitioner, Ronald recognized the urgent need for comprehensive cancer support that bridges medical knowledge with emotional care.

What began as a personal mission to help patients in his community has grown into a dedicated organization serving cancer warriors across the region.

"I saw patients struggling not just with medical challenges, but with fear, isolation and lack of information. Brave Hearts exists to change that reality."

- Ronald Aduda, Founder
